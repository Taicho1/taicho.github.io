About Us

We, the students of Kenyatta University MedTech, are passionate about exploring new avenues in healthcare and technology.
Our mission is to bring together diverse minds from various fields to collaborate on innovative ideas, fostering a culture of
creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving among students. This platform will serve as a central hub for sharing ideas,
resources, and progress updates. We aim to empower our members with the knowledge and skills necessary to make a tangible
impact in the healthcare sector.
